About Health Journal

Our Mission

Health Journal is a comprehensive personal health tracking application designed to help you take control of your health journey. Our mission is to provide a secure, user-friendly platform where you can document your symptoms, manage medications, and maintain a detailed record of your health information.

Features
  • Journal Entries: Track symptoms, moods, and health observations
  • Medication Management: Keep track of your medications, dosages, and schedules
  • Document Upload: Store important medical documents securely
  • Privacy First: Your health data is private and secure
Privacy & Security

We take your privacy seriously. All health information is encrypted and stored securely. Your data belongs to you, and we will never share it without your explicit consent. For more details, please see our Privacy Policy.
